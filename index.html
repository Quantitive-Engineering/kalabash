About Kalabash

Nestled in the heart of Qurum, Kalabash Restaurant & Lounge is more than just a dining destination — it is a celebration of Muscat's vibrant culture and culinary diversity. With sweeping views of the city and the coastline, we offer a sanctuary where modern elegance meets traditional Omani warmth.

From our beginnings as a local gem to our status as a premier lounge, our mission has remained the same: to provide an unforgettable sensory experience through bold flavors, soulful music, and an atmosphere that feels like home.
